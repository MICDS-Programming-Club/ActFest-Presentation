Information

Who can join Programming Club?

Anybody can join Programming Club! No experience is required! Even if you're just interested, don't hesitate to register!

What will Programming Club do?

We will do everything from web development, to drone swarms, to hackathons, and more! Your experience is what you make of it.

When does Programming Club meet?

Programming Club will usually meet for the full activities period every Day 6, but we can also communicate via an application called Slack. Slack is a messaging app comparable to Skype or Discord, which allows us to work on projects together outside of school.
